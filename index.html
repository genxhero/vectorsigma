Hedronite Attack!!

Instructions and Controls

  • A: Move Left
  • D: Move RIght
  • P: Throw a punch (unlimited uses, 3 hits to kill a block)
  • K: Throw a ki blast (limited uses, kills a block instantly)
  • Space: Pause
Objective: Dodge blocks and collect Orbs of Power.

YOU WON!

Having gathered enough power, Kam stormed the throne room of the King Hedronite. Much to his surprise, the king had a hostage: an elf maiden with a bored expression on her face. This was Lluya, ambassador of the Syline forest, who had been captured about a month after Kam's first foray into Hedro City. What her errand was or how she was captured is unimportant. After a fierce battle Kam was victorious - striking the final blow not in anger, and not in the name of vengeance, but for justice. Kam emerges from Hedron with a new mission: return Llyua to her people. Long story short: he dropped her off at home, was granted the honorable title of elf-friend, and rewarded with a sweet magic cloak.

What new adventures await the heroic monk? Only time will tell!
